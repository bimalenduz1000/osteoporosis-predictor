What is Osteoporosis?

Osteoporosis is a condition where bones become weak and brittle, making them more prone to fractures. It often develops silently over years and is influenced by age, gender, lifestyle, and genetics.

About Us

This app predicts the probability of Osteoporosis based on health, lifestyle, and family history factors. It uses a trained XGBoost model.
